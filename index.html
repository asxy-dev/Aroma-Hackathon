How BloodConnect Works

Our platform makes blood donation simple, safe, and efficient through advanced technology and community-driven approach.

Easy Registration

Quick signup process with social login options. Complete your profile with medical history and preferences.

Smart Matching

AI-powered matching system connects compatible donors with recipients based on location, blood type, and urgency.

Real-time Notifications

Instant SMS and email alerts when your blood type is needed nearby or when you're eligible to donate again.

Verified Requests

All blood requests are verified by registered hospitals and medical professionals to ensure authenticity.

Gamified Experience

Earn badges, track your impact, and see how many lives you've helped save through your donations.

Donation Scheduling

Book appointments at nearby blood banks or donation drives with calendar integration and reminders.
